Machinery Supply

We deal with all types of agro-machinery supplies, fruits and vegetable processing machinery, grains and pulses processing machinery, oil distillation and extraction machinery, juice plant machinery, etc to name a few.

Turnkey Projects

Consulting turnkey project is our forte. This includes project planning, layout planning, machine drawing, commissioning and trial run. Also included are PEB structure and civil work, electrical panel and electrification for machines only.

Services

Our services includes offering on project consulting, plant and machinery drawing, repairs and maintenance, project and machinery Vastu compliances, etc.

PEB Structure

Consulting on Pre-Engineering Building(PEB) structure for various plants and machineries happens to be our core expertise. Be it industrial buildings, industrial sheds or warehouses, we have successfully delivered all upto our clients satisfaction.
